Your Money.

The fiscal system allows you to decide the destination of 7% of your taxes. If you don't use it, the government decides for you.

Keyah is the fiscal activism tool connecting your resources with audited Civil Associations.

Intelligence and Legal Fiscal Responsibility

The Problem

Your taxes enter a "black box." You don't know if they end up in healthcare, education, or corruption.

The Keyah Solution

You exercise your right to deduct 7%. Keyah diverts that flow from the government towards causes you audit.

The Keyah Trust Standard

We don't accept just anyone. To be on Keyah, an association must pass the strictest screening in Mexico.

  • Authorized Donee (SAT)

    Mandatory requirement for your donation to be tax-deductible.

  • AIT Accreditation (Cemefi)

    Guarantee of Institutionality and Transparency.

Privacy Engineering: GDPR by Design

We don't want your data. Keyah is designed with a Local-First architecture. No intermediate servers reading your fiscal information.

Built with Flutter

High-performance native code. Compiled directly for ARM (Android/iOS), ensuring fluidity without vulnerable web layers.

Local SQLite

The entire association database lives inside your phone. Your searches and selections never travel across the internet.

X

No Cloud Backend

By eliminating the central server for user data, we eliminate the risk of massive leaks. Absolute compliance with GDPR and privacy.
